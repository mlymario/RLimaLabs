Welcome to RLab


The RLab is a multidisciplinary laboratory interested to study flow and heat transfer phenomena happen at macro, micro and nanoscale levels.


The main propose of the RLad is to develop innovative and valuable methodologies, fluids, materials, prototypes and patents in order to contribute to the progress of the science and society in several areas of knowledge such as: microfluidics, biofluids, nanofluids, thermofluids, blood flow, biomodels, PDMS devices and organs-on-a-chip.


Students in the RLad lab can experience the true beauty of the research, improve their skills and make valuable contributions to the progress of the science and society. You are welcome to visit the RLad lab anytime.


The RLab belongs to Dep. of Mechanical Engineering at Minho University and is located at the Campus of Azurém, Guimarães, Portugal. During the years, the RLab collaborated with several International Institutions such as: Tohoku Univ. (Japan), Extremadura Univ.(Spain), Tokyo Medical and Dental Univ.(Japan), Harvard Medical School (USA), MIT (USA), Strathclyde Univ.(UK) and Bristol Univ.(UK).
